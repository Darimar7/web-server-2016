About Us

CESAM originated from the restlessness and preocupation of a multidisciplinary group of college students for the protection and conservation of our environment. We are the Student Chapter of the Marine Society, better known as SAM, for its acronym in Spanish. SAM is a nonprofit organization recognized in Puerto Rico and in the Caribbean for their research and projects related to the cultivation of endangered species of corals such as: Acropora palmata, Acropora cervicornis, among others.

Our Vision

The Marine Society, as well as its student chapter, have as vision the Puerto Rican citizenship awareness about the conservation of our marine, coastal and terrestrial ecosystems. We focus in the monitoring the state of coral reefs of the north and east of the island, including Culebra. Also, we focus in conserving our coasts organizing beach and underwater clean-ups.
